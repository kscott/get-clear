# Get Clear

I want to be doing better. And I want to share how I'm doing better.

That's the whole thing. Not a product pitch. Not a feature list. A person who found something that helped and couldn't keep it to themselves.

---

## The itch

I'm a manager. Collaborative, busy, juggling commitments to a lot of people. Every meeting promised, every email owed, every follow-up I said I'd send — they lived in my head, which meant they lived in the way of everything else.

The people I work with deserve better than "I meant to get back to you." So do I.

The developers have Cursor, Copilot, a thousand AI coding tools built by people who understand their problem. The person herding cats, keeping the process going, trying to honor every commitment while the calendar fills and the inbox grows — they get nothing tailored to them.

I had that problem. I built for it.

---

## What it is

Get Clear is a suite of command-line tools that connects Claude directly to the things you already use — Calendar, Reminders, Contacts, Mail, Messages.

You tell Claude what needs to happen. It handles the machinery. The meeting gets added. The email goes out. The reminder is set. The follow-through is done.

Not a new system to learn. Not another app to check. Just you, Claude, and everything handled.

The tools cover the full loop:

| | |
|---|---|
| **Contacts** | The people at the center of it all |
| **Reminders** | The promises you've made |
| **Calendar** | Your commitments in time |
| **Mail** | Getting communication out |
| **Messages** | The quick word, without breaking flow |

---

## The tools

- [reminders-cli](https://github.com/kscott/reminders-cli) — Add, change, complete, and find reminders
- [calendar-cli](https://github.com/kscott/calendar-cli) — Query and manage your calendar
- [contacts-cli](https://github.com/kscott/contacts-cli) — Keep your people current
- [mail-cli](https://github.com/kscott/mail-cli) — Send and find email via Fastmail
- [sms-cli](https://github.com/kscott/sms-cli) — Send messages via Messages.app

Each tool is a standalone Swift CLI for macOS, built to work with Claude as the interface. They can be used independently or together.

## How it actually feels

**The follow-up you almost forgot**
> "Remind me to follow up with Sarah if I haven't heard from her by Friday afternoon"

Done. You're still present. Nothing slipped.

**The meeting you just promised**
> "Add a 30-minute check-in with Marcus next Tuesday at 2pm"

On the calendar. You kept your word in under ten seconds.

**Before a big week**
> "What does my week look like? Show me just work stuff."

Not an app to configure. Just the answer.

**The context before the hard conversation**
> "Show me David's contact and find any emails from him in the last month"

You go in with context. You're not scrambling.

**The thing that needed to go out**
> "Send Alex an email, subject 'Following up on Thursday', body — just checking the proposal landed okay"

Sent. One thought, one action, done.
