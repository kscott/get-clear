Welcome to Get Clear

This installer puts five command-line tools on your Mac:

  • reminders — add, complete, and track your commitments
  • calendar — see and manage your calendar
  • contacts — keep contacts current
  • mail — send email from the terminal
  • sms — send messages via Messages.app

Tools are installed to /usr/local/bin. After installation, you'll connect your Fastmail account and name your calendar groups.

The whole setup takes about five minutes.
